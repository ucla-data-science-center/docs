# Getting Started with UCLA Dataverse

UCLA Dataverse is available to UCLA-affiliated researchers, faculty, staff, and students. Use this guide to log in, create your first dataverse, and begin depositing data.

---

# Accounts and Login

## What you need
You need an active UCLA Logon ID that uses Shibboleth authentication.  
If you need help, visit the UCLA Identity and Accounts Manager:  
<https://accounts.iam.ucla.edu/>

You can also contact the Data Science Center for support.

## What happens when I leave UCLA?
When your affiliation ends, your login access may change.  
The DSC can help you transfer datasets, update contacts, or move ownership to a collaborator.  
Email **dsc@ucla.edu** for assistance.

# Creating Your Dataverse and Datasets

## Dataverse or Dataset: What is the difference?
![Dataverse Diagram]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/Dataverse-Diagram.png)

- A **dataverse** is a container for datasets or other dataverses  
- A **dataset** holds data files, code, documentation, and metadata

---

# Create a Dataverse

![Create a Dataverse]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/new-dataverse.png)

Complete the required fields:

- **Host Dataverse:** Select where it will live  
- **Dataverse Name:** Full title of the collection  
- **Affiliation:** Department, project, lab, or journal  
- **Identifier:** Short URL identifier (no spaces or special characters)  
- **Category:** Type of dataverse (researcher, project, organization)  
- **Email:** Contact address for the dataverse  
- **Description:** Short summary, supports limited HTML

Contact the DSC for help formatting descriptions.
