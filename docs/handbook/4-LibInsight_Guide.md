## Why We Use LibInsight

By using LibInsight, we provide the Library with data on how we operate and how often we receive consultations. This data is important because it demonstrates that the DataSquad produces work and is actively consulting.

## What to Record and When

After performing any consultation (in person or online), you must fill out the LibInsight Form.

| Item to Record | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Consultations** | After performing any consultation (in person or online). |
| **Internal Meetings** | Record any meeting that you organize within the DataSquad or DSC. |
| **External Communications** | Emails between you and a client can also be recorded. |

Our goal is to leave a record of the work that we have done and provided in as many forms as possible.

## How to Use LibInsight

1.  **Go to the Website:** Access the detailed entry form here:
    [https://ucla.libinsight.com/detailed](https://ucla.libinsight.com/detailed)

2.  **Log In:** Access the current login credentials (Email and Password) directly from the secure Google Doc below.
    * **Credentials Document:** [LibInsight Account Login Info](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1KAhtvfEQKKK487figML_6WBioTfQQa0Ui6Wb3j35YjM/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.vweojpmlchp1)

3.  **Fill Out the Form:**
    

    * **Date and Time:** Fill out the Event Date and Time.
    * **Duration:** Fill out the duration in minutes.
    * **Location:** Select **Data Science Center** under the "Unit/Location" section.
    * **Service Point:** Select **Off Desk** under the "Service point" section.
    * **Interaction Mode:** Select either **Online - synchronous** or **In-person**.
    * **Question Type:** Typically select **Research Assistance** or **Technical**, but select another option if appropriate.
    * **Session Focus:** Usually one of the following:
        * `Gather and organize information and data`
        * `Analyze and evaluate...`
        * `Use information and data ethically`
    * **Scheduled or Drop-In:** Match the service point selection to **'Drop-in'** or **'Scheduled consultation'**.

4.  **Submit:** Click submit once all fields are complete.
